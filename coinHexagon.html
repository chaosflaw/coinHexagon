This simple puzzle was invented by Henry Dudeney. The objective is to form a hexagon given the initial positions, by sliding each coin, with the following rules:

  • A move is only valid if a coin slides to touch at least two other coins.
  • A coin surrounded by all five others cannot move through the gap (i.e. you cannot push other coins while moving).
  • While getting to the hexagon can be easy, the objective is to do it in three moves.
